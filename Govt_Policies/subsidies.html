Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

Provides short-term credit limits up to $\text{Rs. 3}$ lakh for crop production, post-harvest expenses, and working capital for allied activities at low interest (4% effective rate for prompt payers).

Official Application Portal

PM-KISAN: Direct Income Support

The flagship scheme providing $\text{Rs. 6,000}$ per year directly to the bank accounts of eligible farmer families, ensuring financial security for input purchases.

Check Status / Registration

Farm Mechanization Subsidies

Subsidies (typically 40%-50%) are provided for purchasing modern machinery like Tractors, Rotavators, Seed Drills, and Drones to reduce labor and increase efficiency.

State-wise Subsidy Links

PM-Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana (Pension)

A social security scheme for small and marginal farmers providing a minimum fixed pension of $\text{Rs. 3,000}$ per month after attaining 60 years of age. Contributory scheme.
