These schemes are key to modernizing farming, ensuring financial security, and promoting sustainability.

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Focus: Direct Income Support.
Detail: Provides $\text{Rs. 6,000}$ annually in three installments to landholding farmer families. Ensures basic income stability.

2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Focus: Crop Insurance & Risk Mitigation.
Detail: Comprehensive insurance cover against natural risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest. Low farmer premium rates make it highly accessible.

3. Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Focus: Post-Harvest Management & Infrastructure.
Detail: A $\text{Rs. 1}$ lakh crore debt financing facility for building cold storage, warehouses, and primary processing units through interest subvention and credit guarantee.

4. Digital Agriculture Mission (DAM)

Focus: Digital & Precision Farming.
Detail: Developing 'Agri Stack' with unique Farmer IDs and the Krishi Decision Support System (Krishi-DSS) to enable data-driven farm management and services.

5. National Mission on Natural Farming (NMNF)

Focus: Sustainable & Chemical-Free Farming.
Detail: Promotes natural farming methods, providing incentives of $\text{Rs. 4,000}$ per acre per year for two years to encourage large-scale adoption.

6. Formation & Promotion of 10,000 FPOs

Focus: Farmer Empowerment & Collective Bargaining.
Detail: Aims to create $\text{10,000}$ new Farmer Producer Organizations to integrate small farmers into the market supply chain, improving price realization.

7. National Mission on Edible Oils - Oil Palm (NMEO-OP)

Focus: Self-Reliance in Edible Oils.
Detail: Financial and technical support to boost domestic production of oilseeds, with a special emphasis on the North-Eastern States and the Andaman region.

8. Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Focus: Irrigation & Water Use Efficiency.
Detail: The goal is 'Per Drop More Crop' by promoting micro-irrigation (drip and sprinkler) and expanding the area under assured protective irrigation.

9. National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

Focus: Market Access & Price Transparency.
Detail: Electronic trading platform linking APMC mandis across India to create a unified national market, ensuring better price discovery for farmers' produce.

10. Soil Health Card (SHC) Scheme

Focus: Soil Fertility & Balanced Nutrient Use.
Detail: Provides farmers with a report on the nutrient status of their soil every 2 years, recommending customized dosages of fertilizers for optimal crop yield.
